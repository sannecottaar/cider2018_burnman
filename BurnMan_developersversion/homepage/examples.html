How to try BurnMan out:

Go to test.burnman.org or:
  • go to https://cloud.sagemath.com and create an account
  • create a new project, name it whatever you want, make it private, hit create.
  • click on your project to open it
  • hit "new", paste in "https://github.com/geodynamics/burnman.git", click "from the web" (this will install burnman)
  • Go to "file", "open" and navigate to the folder "burnman/contrib/ipython" and open one of the ipython example projects.
  • You can also copy the files to create your own programs, or upload other ipython files.
